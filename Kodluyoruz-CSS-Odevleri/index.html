Youtube

Youtube is a platform where people can share videos from all over the world free of charge. It was first launched on 14th February 2005 by Steven Chen, Chad Hurley and Jawed Karim. It was owned by Google on 9th October 2006 and the acquiring costed around $1.65 billion. Youtube has more than 2.5 billion monthly users. As of June 2022, users watch more than one billion hours of videos each day and more than 500 hours of content were uploaded to Youtube per minute. You can visit the website by clicking on here.
