Youtube

Youtube is a platform where people can share videos from all over the world free of charge. It was first launched on 14th February 2005 by Steven Chen, Chad Hurley and Jawed Karim. It was owned by Google on 9th October 2006 and the acquiring costed around $1.65 billion. Youtube has more than 2.5 billion monthly users. As of June 2022, users watch more than one billion hours of videos each day and more than 500 hours of content were uploaded to Youtube per minute. You can visit the website by clicking on here.

Twitter

Twitter is a platform that allows people to share their thoughts freely. It was launched by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone and Evan Williams in July 2006. People can 'tweet' what they want, make live streams by Twitter Space and follow what they desire by creating lists. 350 thousand tweets are tweeted on Twitter per minute and it has more than 450 million monthly users. Twitter is acquired by Elon Musk by a record amount of $44 billions. Musk is planning to make Twitter an 'everything app' like WeChat. You can visit the website by clicking on here.
